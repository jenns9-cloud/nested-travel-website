Subscription Tiers

Nested Travel offers two subscription tiers:

Free Tier:

  • 1 active trip at a time

  • PDF export

Pro Tier ($6.99/month):

  • Unlimited trips
  • Unlimited saved places
  • Unlimited AI trip generation and regeneration

Free tier users receive 1 AI-generated itinerary per calendar month. This limit resets automatically on the 1st of each month. Unused AI credits do not roll over. Pro subscribers have unlimited AI usage. AI-generated itineraries are suggestions only and may not reflect real-time availability.
